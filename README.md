## Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end airline analytics solution using SQL, Excel, and Power BI. It focuses on analyzing flight operations, delays, passenger trends, and revenue performance to derive actionable business insights.

## Objective

To identify operational inefficiencies and revenue patterns in airline data and provide data-driven recommendations for improving performance and customer experience.

 Tools & Technologies

SQL (PostgreSQL) – Data extraction & transformation

Excel – Data validation & cleaning

Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard

## Dataset

The dataset includes:

Flights data (routes, delays, cancellations)

Airlines information

Passenger bookings

Revenue calculations

## Key KPIs

Total Flights Operated

Total Revenue Generated

Total Passengers

On-Time Performance (%)

Average Delay (minutes)

Cancellation Rate (%)

## Key Insights

A small number of routes contribute significantly to total revenue

Certain airports consistently experience higher delays

On-time performance varies across airlines

Flight delays increase during high-demand periods

Cancellations impact both revenue and passenger experience

## Business Recommendations

Optimize operations at high-delay airports

Increase frequency on high-performing routes

Improve scheduling during peak demand periods

Reduce cancellations through better planning
